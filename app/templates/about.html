How It Works

  • We analyze URL patterns and metadata (scheme, domain, path, query).
  • We compute a risk score and show signals that affected the decision.
  • We store scan history per user, with export and basic trends.

Important Note

This is a helper tool. Always verify critical links independently and avoid entering sensitive data on unknown sites.
